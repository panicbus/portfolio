    • Scenic 49

    Ruby on Rails | HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript | Isotope.js | MapBox | AWS | Paperclip Ruby Gem




    Map View with Station Markers



    All Checkins Page



    Mobile view

    On The Road: The Scenic 49 is an art project concieved by Peter Foucault and Chris Treggiari where the artists hiked the famous 49 Mile Scenic Drive in San Francisco and hosted interactive pop-up screenprinting installations at 9 different locations around the city.

    At each "Station" the local passersby stopped to get some art and talk about how their neighborhood is changing. Photos of them along with their checkins would be uploaded and saved to the web app's database for later perusal at an art opening at the end of their hike.

    All the checkins and photos are archived on the site in the "All Checkins" page and can be filtered by Station.

    This project can be cloned and reproduced for any city in the world and is highly encouraged.
