    Scenic 49

     Built with: Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Isotope.js, MapBox, AWS, Paperclip Gem



    Map View with Station Markers



    All Checkins Page



    Mobile view

    On The Road: The Scenic 49 is the interactive online map component of an art project conceived by Peter Foucault and Chris Treggiari. Over the course of three days the artists hiked the famous 49 Mile Scenic Drive in San Francisco and hosted interactive screenprinting stations around the city.

    The artists interacted with local passersby to talk about the change in their neighborhood in exchange for art. Photos along with their thoughts were saved to the web app's database for later perusal at an art opening in March of 2014.

    This project can be cloned and reproduced for any city in the world.
